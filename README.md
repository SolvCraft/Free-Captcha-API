Boost your web form security without sacrificing performance. The **Free Open-Source Captcha API** is an ultra-fast, stateless Captcha API designed for modern web applications. Protect your login, signup, and contact forms completely free from bot abuse while maintaining exceptional speed and prioritizing user privacy.

## Why Choose This Free Open-Source Captcha API?

### 🚀 Blazing Fast Edge Performance
Built specifically for speed. The generator and validator run seamlessly, offering low-latency responses for every user. Form submissions are verified in milliseconds, ensuring a seamless user experience that never blocks your core application logic.

No creepy tracking cookies, no invasive user-fingerprinting. Our validator is entirely stateless and deeply respects user privacy. Sensitive data and IPs are automatically masked to ensure full compliance with privacy standards.

### 💰 100% Free & Open-Source
Tired of paying premium enterprise fees for basic bot protection? Our **Free Open-Source Captcha API** gives you enterprise-grade reliability with zero monthly costs. You own your data, and the architecture is designed to be fully open source.

Understand your traffic without compromising user identity. Get insights into where your form submissions are coming from, blocked rates, and execution speeds—all heavily masked for privacy compliance and safely stored.

Works with any framework: React, Vue, Next.js, Angular, or plain HTML. Simply request a captcha from the `/generate` endpoint, show the fast-loading SVG image, and validate the user submission via `/verify`. No bloated client-side SDKs required.
